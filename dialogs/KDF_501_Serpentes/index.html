Serpentes

HeroTextAudio
Serpentes(scolding) You dare to address me, novice? Return to your work.
MeI've got one more question ...
Serpentes(furiously) You do not seem to want to understand. I decide when I see fit to talk to you. Who do you think you are?
SerpentesAnd now get back to work!
SerpentesYour presence here demonstrates not only your lack of respect, but also your stupidity, novice!
MeI heard that you passed the Test of Fire.
Serpentes(smugly) In all humility, I can tell you this. Everyone else who has attempted this is dead.
Serpentes(condescendingly) You really should not attempt to take this test. Your spirit is weak. Better to serve in the monastery and perhaps you will be chosen in a few years.
MeI am going to pass the test.
SerpentesIf it is the will of Innos, then you shall. But if not - then you will fail.
MeI am ready to face your test, Master.
SerpentesSo, you want to put yourself to the test. Only a courageous novice demands the test. But courage is not all that you need.
SerpentesAre you clever enough to pass the test? Are you strong enough to meet the dangers that await you?
SerpentesIf not, you will pay for it with your life.
SerpentesAnd now hear your test: In the Place of Stones, seek the one who was never born - find him who once was summoned.
SerpentesOvercome the one who cannot be overcome - measure yourself against the living rock, fight with the undying stone - and destroy it.
MeLiving rock ... ? What kind of a creature would that be?
SerpentesI have told you everything. (mockingly) Or do you find this test too difficult?
SerpentesNow you will learn what it means to take the Test of Fire - I will answer no more of your questions.
Serpentes(contemptibly) Go and carry out your task, novice!
MeI have defeated the golem.
SerpentesWhat? You have actually done it? But without the hammer of Innos, you would never have been able to destroy the golem.
Serpentes(triumphantly) But now you have betrayed yourself! It was you who killed Garwig!
SerpentesRECEIVE THE PUNISHMENT FOR THE MURDER OF A SERVANT OF INNOS!!!
Serpentes(regretfully) Nevertheless, I must admit that you have fulfilled the task I have given you.
SerpentesAnd I had best take the hammer myself.
MeIs there anything else you want to tell me?
SerpentesNo. Not at the moment, Brother.
SerpentesDismissed. You know what you have to do.
SerpentesNot so fast. I have another task for you.
Me(sighs) And that is?
SerpentesNow that you belong to the Brotherhood of Fire, you must also fulfill the duties that come with being one of us.
SerpentesThe church is not only concerned with the affairs of magic - it is also the final authority in the administration of justice, as you know.
SerpentesThe latest case brought before opens up a bottomless abyss and must be dealt with once and for all.
SerpentesA mercenary for hire of your stripe could be of use to us in this matter.
MeIsn't that the militia's business?
SerpentesThe militia is responsible for the city alone. This affair has farther-reaching consequences.
MeI've got enough on my plate as it is. Go find somebody else.
Serpentes(angrily) That is outrageous. You will never reach the upper circles of magic if you are not willing to do the Brotherhood a service.
SerpentesI hereby reprimand you. I hope this will not happen again - else it will go badly with you.
MeWhat's this about?
SerpentesThere is someone in the city who has sold falsified shares in the ore mines of the former penal colony.
SerpentesThese papers are utterly worthless and of no importance, really. Still, they have the potential of stirring up trouble among the people, which we cannot afford to happen right now.
SerpentesThe swindler is supposed to have approached all the most reputable traders in the city and the country, and has evidently even managed to sell his counterfeit mining shares to some of them.
SerpentesFind this evildoer and bring him before this panel. In such hard times we must not suffer swindlers and plunderers.
SerpentesWe must make an example of him in order to deter the rest of the mob.
MeI shall try to clear up the matter.
SerpentesOne thing more. When you visit those traders, do not let them know that you are looking for mining shares.
SerpentesI think they probably want to sell them on and certainly would no longer offer them to you if they realized that you are dealing with them on behalf of the church, you see?
MeYes.
SerpentesThen get to work. I wish you much success, Brother.
MeWhom should I kill?
SerpentesFor Innos' sake. No one, of course. This business demands a bit more finesse, you rude lout.
SerpentesSalandril, the alchemist from the upper end of town, is guilty of a serious crime and must be punished for it.
SerpentesHowever, he has refused to come to the monastery to be judged. Bring him here.
SerpentesHow you accomplish this does not matter to us. You will, of course, be rewarded.
MeNo problem. I'll take care of it.
SerpentesGood. We expect to hear from you in a few days.
MeI've been able to retrieve a mining share.
MeI've been able to retrieve a few mining shares.
SerpentesVery good. You must remove them all from circulation. This is poison for the people. Bring them all to me.
SerpentesHere. I will give you the necessary means.
MeThose were all, I think.
SerpentesWell done. You have earned a reward.
SerpentesTake this protective amulet. It will help you along the paths you have yet to tread.
SerpentesThis is really the last one now, right?
MeI know who brought those mining shares into circulation. Salandril did, the alchemist from the upper quarter of town.
SerpentesThen bring him here. We have things to discuss with him.
MeSalandril is here, in the monastery.
SerpentesWell done. We shall deal with him later.
SerpentesHere is your reward. And not another word about this, understood?
MeSalandril is dead.
SerpentesWell, then his deed has not gone unpunished. May Innos have mercy on his poor soul.
