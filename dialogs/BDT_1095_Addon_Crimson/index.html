CrimsonBecause, just imagine: one fine evening Raven comes along and shoves a coin die in my hands.
CrimsonOut of one single gold nugget, I can mint a heap of coins - I'm so good that no one can tell the difference!

CrimsonWell, I don't know you at all, but I'll make you a special price. For one nugget I'll give you...

CrimsonHmm...no. That's the standard price EVERYONE gets.
MeI thought that was a special price.
CrimsonIt is. Everyone here is special to me.

CrimsonBut you don't have any gold nuggets on you.

MeWhat do you know about Raven?
CrimsonYou won't believe it. I was there. I saw what he did in the tomb!
Crimson(afraid) He mumbled and called some strange words. Over and over...
Crimson(loudly) And then a blazing light came out of the tomb and I heard the SCREAM.

CrimsonRaven spoke with it, they conversed - Raven and THAT voice!
CrimsonI can't remember what they said - I just know that I could only master my limbs again many hours later, back in the camp.
